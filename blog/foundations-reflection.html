Foundation Reflection

1. What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

I have learnt that I like taking time and try to hold on to the problem if I do not understand.
But also I have learnt that things are too much for me, I tends to give up instead of moving on.
I also learnt that I need to and want to improve my EQ skills.

2. What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

The values, empathy, and self-awareness will provide motivation, and help thinking from the other's point of view, and indetify my own strength and weakness which can help managing stress and burn out.

3. What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

The condept of Neuroplasticity. It has been helping me a lot whenever I want to give up.

4. What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

It was challenging thinking and identifying my own identity and values.

5. Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

I believe that the human skill is very important especially in team environment. And the culture they make carries on and it is hard to change. By practicing these skills, I will be happier and more efficient at work but also make the team environment nicer.

6. Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

Although it was unusual study, I found it quite useful. It helped me understand myself better and quite interesting how I can view and analyse the situation. It definitely helped me when I was going through the hard time.
