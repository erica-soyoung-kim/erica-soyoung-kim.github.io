JavaScript - DOM

1. An analogy to describe JavaScript and its relationship to HTML and CSS.

Imagine your webpage is a house, HTML is the structure of the house.
CSS is the interior design, such as colour, font, etc.
JavaScript is like the function of the house, such as electricity and plumbing. In webpage, it represents interactive form or animations.


2. Explain control flow and loops using an example process from everyday life, for example, 'waking up' or 'brushing your teeth' (but not those).

Control flow is like the steps you follow.
For example, let's say you are going to work some days in a week.
When you wake up, if it is your working day, then you go to work.
Or else (if it is NOT your working day), then you do not go to work, stay at home and do some housework.


Loop is like repeating tasks.
For example, you exercise everyday for 30 minutes until you reach desired weight.
Everyday, you loop (exercise) until the condition is fulfilled (reach desired weight).
