Tell your non-tech friend a story about a time you:

1. were blocked on a simple problem:

During the kata task, I was stuck on accessing object element of different object. I ended up ask chat GPT how to access and it gave me a clear answer. I thought chatGPT was genius as it knew exactly what I was asking for. I learnt not to be afraid on searching online or use resources.

2. elegantly solved a problem.

During the deBee tasks, I really could not figure out how to ignore the case sensitivity. Even searching on Google, it did not give me a clear answer. I checked on the discord, someone has asked about the same question and was answered. I had a look at the code, understood why and how they had this approach and solved it again. I was getting frustrated when I could not find the answer online, but I learnt ask group is very helpful also for those people who have the same problem as me.
